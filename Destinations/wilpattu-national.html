Best Time to Visit

Wilpattu can be visited year-round, but the dry season (May to early September) offers the best wildlife viewing, as animals concentrate near the remaining water sources. Rainy periods, especially September to December, bring lush greenery but can make roads muddy and harder to traverse. Highly Rated Nearby Restaurants

As with many large parks, the region around Wilpattu has fewer high-profile restaurants. Most dining options are provided by local lodges and safari camps near the entrances. Travelers often choose to dine in towns like Anuradhapura before or after their park visit.

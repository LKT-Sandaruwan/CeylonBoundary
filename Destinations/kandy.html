Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit Kandy are December to March, when rainfall is minimal and the weather is pleasant ‚Äî perfect for sightseeing, cultural exploration, and serene walks around the city.

Important Travel Notes

  • Attire: Lightweight, breathable clothing; shoulders and knees should be covered at religious sites.
  • Footwear: Comfortable walking shoes are a must for temple visits and exploring around Kandy Lake.
  • Sun Protection: Sunscreen, hats, and sunglasses are essential during the day.
  • Hydration: Carry water, especially when visiting during the warmer months.
  • Moving Around: Tuk-tuks are the most convenient way for short trips between attractions such as the Temple of the Tooth, markets, and Botanical Gardens.
